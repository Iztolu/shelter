SERVICES

Owing to need for specialization, the firm has four department providing services in Architecture, Design, Construction and Project Management

  • ARCHITECTURAL CONSULTANCY SERVICES
  • DESIGN CONSULTANCY SERVICES
  • BUILDING CONSTRUCTION SERVICES
  • PROJECT MANAGEMENT CONSULTANCY SERVICES

Apart from meeting clients’ functional and non functional needs, our design methodologies enable us to deliver clients’ projects within cost, time and quality.

Our design methodology includes the following;

Preliminary design
a. Detail analysis of clients’ brief
b. Preparing and illustration of outline proposal
c. Cost analysis
d. Full scale presentation drawings
e. Report with estimated cost for client’s approval

Detail design and production drawings
a. Detail working drawings with specifications
b. Site plan in details
c. Detail structural drawings
d. Detail mechanical and electrical drawings
e. Bill of quantities and cost checks
f. Tender procedure/ Award

Site Supervision
a. Final detail drawings
b. Site supervision - Site meetings/Site Visits
c. Final report

It important to note here that the above methodology enables us meet our clients’ functional requirements as well as ensuring that proposed development is delivered within cost and time 


The following are services offered by our construction department:

a. Construction of Buildings
b. Construction of Roads
c. Water Reticulation
d. Electrical works
e. Erosion Control
f. Landscaping 

At Shelter Consult, the management view success of Clients’ proposal as the totality of the effectiveness of managerial skills adopted from Conceptualization, Construction and throughout Life Span of the project.

Our Project Management Consultancy Services include,

a. Preparation of clients’ brief
b. Preparation of project feasibility study
c. Advice the client on the selection of Consultants
d. Advice the client on the selection of Contractors
e. Advice the client on payments for work down to contractors
f. Development of maintenance schedule
g. Monitoring and Evaluation Consultancy

and other related Project Management Services.
